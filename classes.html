Classes

We teach rigging classes through Paradise Highline Community. There is a Rigging Fundamentals class, suitable for first time highliners through people who have been rigging for about a year. There is also an ISA Prep class, aimed at experienced highline riggers, that covers most of the material that's tested as part of the ISA Rigger's Certification. Each class is a 2.5 day intensive, spanning Friday afternoon to Sunday afternoon. The classes are located in either the Front Range of Colorado, near Boulder, or near Moab, Utah. There is generally one of each class in the Spring and one of each class in the Fall.
